## Test Instructions

### Auth Flow
1. Launch app - should see login screen
2. Tap "Sign Up" to create account
3. Enter name, email, password (min 6 chars)
4. After sign up, should see main tabs
5. Go to Settings > Sign Out to log out

### Task Creation (Honeydew)
1. Tap Honeydew tab
2. Tap + (FAB) to add task
3. Fill in title, optional due date, priority
4. Save and see task in list
5. Tap checkbox to complete
6. Swipe to delete

### Recipe Creation
1. Tap Recipes tab
2. Tap + (FAB) to add recipe
3. Step 1: Enter title, servings, times
4. Step 2: Add ingredients with quantities
5. Step 3: Add step-by-step instructions
6. View recipe and adjust servings

### Meal Planning
1. Tap Meal Plan tab
2. Use arrows to navigate weeks
3. Tap day to assign recipe
4. Tap X to remove recipe from day

### Grocery Generation
1. Plan meals for the week first
2. Tap Grocery tab
3. Tap "Generate from Meal Plan"
4. Check off items as you shop
5. Add manual items with +

### Settings
1. View profile info
2. Manage pantry staples
3. Sign out when done
